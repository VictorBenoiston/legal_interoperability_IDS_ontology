LegIOn-IDS' Documentation

Welcome to LegIOn-IDS'Documentation. This ontology provides a domain representation of the legal aspects envolved within legal interoperability on the International Data Spaces domain. Fostered by the European Interoperability Framework (EIF) and the Service Contract Ontology (SCO), we provide a framework for the unambiguous representation of an IDS contract, as a means to provide machine-readability, enabling the automation of a few processes regarding contract negotiation and compliance. This ontology was built through the Systematic Approach for Building Ontologies (SABiO), hence, we provide a reference ontology, grounded by the Unified Foundational Ontology (UFO), and its graphical extension (OntoUML), and an operational ontology in Web Ontology Language (OWL).
